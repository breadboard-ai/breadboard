# The Wobble Report — Season 15 Preview

_By Tariq Osei, Senior Correspondent_

The ICWF has finally pulled the trigger on Duo Wobbling, and the community is
split right down the middle — appropriately enough for a sport about balance.

## The Resonance Question

The new fifth category, Resonance, is where things get interesting.
Counter-Wobble — perfectly opposed oscillation — sounds elegant in theory, but
in practice? Nobody's trained for this. I watched Jaxon "Jitter" Vane and his
training partner attempt it at the pre-season exhibition, and let's just say
there were multiple Splorts.

The scoring itself is clear enough: phase lock, amplitude matching, and recovery
coordination. But the weightings haven't been published yet, which means judges
will have enormous discretion in Season 15. Expect controversy.

## The Gleam Gets Louder

Raising the Technical Gleam cap to 9.0 is the quiet bombshell. For years, the
8.0 ceiling meant that Technical rounds were about precision — FRQ consistency,
SYM ratios, clean arcs. Now? A Technical Wobbler who can channel genuine
artistry will have a full extra point of headroom.

I spoke to retired judge Cass Witmer about this: "It changes the entire
preparation strategy for Technical. Athletes used to ignore the Gleam in
Technical because the ceiling was so low. Now they have to train artistry
alongside form. Elara Voss could have scored 39.0+ under these rules."

## Titans on the Disc

The Titan class is overdue. Everyone in the Ultra bracket knew that a 105kg
Wobbler and a 135kg Wobbler were playing completely different games. The wider
Disc for Titans makes sense from a physics standpoint — moment of inertia scales
with mass distribution, and a standard 60cm Disc just doesn't give a 130kg
athlete enough surface.

## My Hot Take

The Legacy Bonus is sentimental nonsense. +0.3 for loyalty? This isn't an
airline frequent flyer programme. But the Stomp Drift crackdown is welcome —
Platform Manipulation at -1.5 is exactly the deterrent needed after the Kenji
Hashimoto incident at Season 14 Worlds.
